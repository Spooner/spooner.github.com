Alpha Channel

Updated Thursday, 15th December 2011

v1.3.0 (Complete)

A game developed in 48 hours for Ludum Dare #18 - Theme was “Enemies as Weapons” (Single player).

Downloads

An extended version was created after the competition (adding boss fights and improved physics and visuals).
